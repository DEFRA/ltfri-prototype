Check the flood risk for an area in England

Use this service to:

  • check the flood risk from flash flooding (surface water), groundwater, reservoirs, rivers and the sea
  • get evidence of the flood risk for insurance purposes
  • get advice about flooding

Before you start

Find out about who is at risk from flooding and the impacts.

Who is at risk

5.9 million properties are at risk of flooding in England and Wales - 1 in 6 homes.

You can still suffer from flooding if you live on a hill or far from a river.

The impacts

£28,000 is the average cost of a home flooding. Anybody can be affected - even if you live in a top floor flat. It can impact your:

  • health - through disease or impact on mental health
  • access to clean water and food supplies
  • gas, electricity and drainage
  • doctor’s surgery or hospital
  • transport - cars, buses and trains
  • school, place of work, community centre or place of worship
