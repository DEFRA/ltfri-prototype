Flood Risk For: BL9 1XX

Surface Water - High Risk: - This area has a greater than 1 in 30 chance of flooding

What is surface water

Surface water flooding happens when heavy rainfall cannot drain away. Over 3 million properties are at risk from surface water in the UK, more than those at risk from rivers and the sea (2.7 million).

How we assess flood risk from surface water

Flood experts use computer models to observe and assess:

  • Rainwater flow and pooling
  • weather patterns
  • local landscape
  • historical data

Accuracy

We assessed the surface water flood risk for this area in 2017. We will do it again in 2022, surface water flooding is difficult to predict as rainfall location and volume are difficult to forecast.

Surface water risk management

Contact your Lead Local Flood Authority who is responsible for managing the risk of flooding from surface water.

They may have more detailed information on the flood risk from surface water.

Rivers & Sea - Medium Risk: - This area has between 1 in 30 and 1 in 100 chance of flooding

How we assess flood risk from rivers and the sea

Flood experts use computer models to observe and assess:

  • rainwater
  • weather patterns
  • tides
  • historical data

Reservoirs - The selected area is at no risk of flooding from reservoirs
