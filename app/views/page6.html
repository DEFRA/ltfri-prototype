Medium risk - rivers and the sea flood

This area has between 1% and 3.3% chance of flooding from rivers or the sea each year.

How we assess the risk

Flood experts use computer models to observe and assess:

  • rainwater
  • weather patterns
  • local landscape
  • historical data

Accuracy

River and sea levels are regularly checked by our network of monitoring stations.

We take into account any flood defences in the area. Flood defences reduce the risk but do not completely stop it.
